About Association


On May 26, 2017, the Sarawak E-commerce Association officially obtained a license from the government registry. At the beginning of the establishment of this association, five major founding purposes were established:

1. Become a bridge between members and the government
2. Assist in entrepreneurship/employment opportunities
3. Create a shared platform
4. Data collection
5. Education

These five major objectives are also the work indicators of the Association. We organize various related courses and symposiums and conduct knowledge education and training for the public. Create an e-commerce environment belonging to Sarawak and connect with domestic and foreign e-commerce platforms. Held Sarawak e-commerce fairs, trade fairs, and other activities. Lead members to cooperate with the government to help implement relevant policies. Guide members on how to become a cross-border e-commerce provider and provide advice on import and export tariffs and laws.

The Association also promotes the creation of a collaborative environment between professionals and institutions in the IT field and entrepreneurs or traditional businesses and seeks solutions to promote new business opportunities. In addition, this Council will collect data through the association platform to help members save more resources and deliver more accurately from the data.

公会简介


2017年5月26日，砂拉越电子商务公会（ Sarawak E-commerce Association）正式获得到政府注册局的执照批准。本会的创立之初便设立了五大创会宗旨：

1. 成为会员与政府沟通桥梁
2. 协助创业/就业机会
3. 打造共享平台
4. 数据采集
5. 教育

这5大宗旨同时也是公会的工作指标，从主办各种相关课程与座谈会，对大众进行电子商务的知识教育培训。打造属于砂拉越的电商环境并与国内外电商平台对接。举办砂拉越电子商务博览会，展销会等等活动。带领会员与政府合作，协助落实相关政策。引导会员如何成为跨境电商，提供进出口关税与法令等咨询。

本会也推动打造IT领域的专才和机构与创业者或传统商家之间的合作环境，共谋对策，促成新商机效应。此外，本会将透过公会平台进行数据采集，以协助会员从数据中可以节省更多资源和更精准的投放。
